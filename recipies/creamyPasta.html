One-Pot Creamy Kimchi Pasta

Description

A rich and filing dish for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 3 thick-cut slices bacon, cut into ½” strips
  • 1 1/2 c.drained kimchi, roughly chopped
  • 3garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 c.water
  • 2 c.half & half
  • 1 lb.dry pasta, such as small shells
  • 1 tbsp.gochujang (optional)
  • 2 tsp.Kosher salt
  • c.freshly grated Parmesan
  • Thinly sliced scallions, for serving
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, cook bacon, stirring occasionally, until bacon is crisp and fat has been rendered, about 10 minutes.
  2. Empty all but about 1 tablespoon bacon fat from the pot, and add kimchi and garlic. Stir to combine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of your pan. Cook until the garlic is fragrant and most of the moisture has evaporated from the kimchi, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add water, half and half, pasta, gochujang if using, and salt. Bring to a boil and cook, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente, about 8 minutes. Remove from heat, add parm, and stir until melted.
  4. To serve, top with scallions and sesame seeds.
